About

Established in 2019, DisruptWorks Technologies as an IT consulting firm that utilizes disruptive technologies along with a wide range of existing platforms to solve client challenges. We specialize in designing and implementing data centers and applications that use microservices and all available new-age technologies such as blockchain. We take pride in being students of the industry and treat every client as consultants there to augment your current IT team. We provide direct project management services utilizing our team of experienced software developers and solution engineers. From a complete on-premise data center analysis and consultation, cloud migration services, and web and mobile application development. Our team provides continuous support in all phases to deliver you a complete IT solution.
